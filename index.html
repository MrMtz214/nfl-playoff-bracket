Pool Rules & Scoring

Read before entering

Entry Fee $25

Winner Take All: One champion takes the entire pot. Entry must be paid to the commissioner before kickoff.

How Points are Tallied

Points are cumulative. You earn points for every correct team you advance through the bracket. If your Wild Card winner also wins the Divisional round, you earn both sets of points!

Wild Card Round 5 PTS per win
Divisional Round 10 PTS per win
Conf. Championship 15 PTS per win
Super Bowl Winner 20 PTS final win

Tiebreaker: If total points are tied, the person who predicted closest to the Total Combined Score of the Super Bowl wins the pot.
