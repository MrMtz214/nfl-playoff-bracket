How Points are Tallied

Points are cumulative. You earn points for every correct selection you advance. If your Wild Card pick wins the Divisional round, you get points for both!

Wild Card Round 5 PTS per win
Divisional Round 10 PTS per win
Conf. Championship 15 PTS per win
Super Bowl Winner 20 PTS final win
